# How Zero Modernized Email

Email has been stuck in the past for far too long. While every other aspect of our digital lives has evolved dramatically, email clients have remained frustratingly stagnant. At Zero Mail, we decided it was time for a complete reimagining.

## The Problem with Traditional Email

Traditional email clients suffer from several fundamental issues:

- **Overwhelming interfaces** that prioritize features over usability
- **Poor search capabilities** that make finding important emails a nightmare  
- **Lack of intelligent organization** leading to cluttered, chaotic inboxes
- **Slow performance** that doesn't match the pace of modern work
- **No AI assistance** for writing, prioritization, or management
